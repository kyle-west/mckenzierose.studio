Like all artists, I started my career at the age of five when I could hold a crayon by myself. Now that I am 23 years old I find myself drawing and cannot get enough of it. I get a thrill from creating brushstrokes that breathe life in my characters and stories. Illustration isn't just my profession it's my passion.

I graduated from Brigham Young University-Idaho with a Bachelor of Arts in Illustration. I have been published several times in the home school program, The Good and the Beautiful, and am currently working on a very large upcoming project for them.

When I'm not working on art, you can find me hiking, jamming to music when I think nobody is looking, sneaking chocolate out of the cupboards, or fancying myself to my sweet husband's homemade Italian food.

If you have any comments on my art or questions feel free to contact me.

I am currently accepting commissions.
