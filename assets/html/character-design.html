She grew up in the ritzy well known city of Las Vegas, Nevada, but she much preferred spending her time out of the city and escaping to the mountains and lakes.

McKenzie earned her Bachelor's degree in 2017 in Illustration at Brigham Young University-Idaho. Since then she has worked as a freelancer. Over the past few years she's worked with a large homeschool program, The Good and The Beautiful, creating young reader chapter books, kindergarten beginner books, and history stories.

McKenzie now lives in Utah Valley with her wonderful husband, Kyle, where she still loves to escape to the mountains, hike, try new restaurants, and add yet another new plant to her home.

McKenzie has always had a love for using watercolors in her work. Here are some finished projects she helped create:

  • Treehouse Town 1 & 2 (Young Reader)
  • Treehouse Town Parts of Speech Game
  • Melissa Across the Fence (Young Reader)
  • Beginner Book Boxes A-D (Beginner Reader)
